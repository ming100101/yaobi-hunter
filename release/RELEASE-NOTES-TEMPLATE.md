# Yaobi Hunter vX.Y.Z — Public Beta

## What is included

- Windows desktop market scanner and multi-timeframe coin detail.
- Telegram delivery monitor with TG-card provenance.
- App-only post-push watches and evidence-first Strategy Lab.
- Local recorder and backward-readable research records.

## Important limitations

- No strategy is a guarantee of profit.
- Experimental and shadow strategies are not live trade instructions.
- The app does not place orders and does not require an exchange API key.
- This build is not code-signed; Windows SmartScreen may warn on first run.
